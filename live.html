Live Monitoring Process

At Defendum Security Services, our live monitoring process is built on a robust protocol that guarantees rapid response and continuous protection. The process includes:

  1. Consultation & Assessment: We begin with an in-depth consultation to evaluate your specific security needs and assess the premises.
  2. Installation & Integration: Our experts install advanced CCTV systems and integrate them seamlessly with our centralized monitoring center.
  3. System Activation: Once the system is tested and fully operational, we activate 24/7 live monitoring to capture real-time data.
  4. Live Surveillance: Our dedicated team continuously monitors the feeds, ensuring that any irregularities are detected immediately.
  5. Rapid Response: On detection of a potential threat, our tactical response teams are mobilized swiftly to secure the area.
  6. Maintenance & Continuous Improvement: Regular system maintenance and periodic reviews help us ensure peak performance and adapt to emerging security challenges.
