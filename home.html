Home Security Tips

Enhance your home's security with these practical tips from our security experts.

Secure Entry Points

Reinforce doors and windows with deadbolt locks, security bars, or pins to prevent forced entry.

Adequate Lighting

Install motion-activated lighting around your property to deter potential intruders.

Visible Security

Display security system signs and stickers to let potential intruders know your home is protected.

Landscape Management

Trim shrubs and trees near windows and doors to eliminate potential hiding spots for intruders.

Key Management

Avoid hiding spare keys outside and consider using smart locks for better access control.

Vacation Security

Create the appearance of occupancy while away by using timers for lights and having mail collected.

Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our home security solutions.

What happens if there's a power outage?

Our security systems include backup batteries that keep your system operational during power outages, ensuring continuous protection.

How long does installation take?

Most residential installations can be completed in 4-6 hours, depending on the system's complexity and your home's size.

Can I control my security system remotely?

Yes, our systems include mobile apps that allow you to arm/disarm your system, view camera feeds, and receive alerts from anywhere.

What happens if an alarm is triggered?

Our monitoring center will immediately contact you to verify the emergency, and if necessary, dispatch emergency services to your home.

Are there monthly monitoring fees?

Yes, we offer various monitoring plans to suit different needs and budgets. We'll explain all options during your consultation.

Can I add components to my system later?

Absolutely! Our systems are designed to be expandable, allowing you to add components as your needs change or as budget allows.
